Structural Analysis

As part of the model loading procedure, doLoad() automatically perform a stoichiometric (structural) analysis of the model. The structural properties of the model are captured in stoichiometric matrix (N), kernel matrix (K) and link matrix (L). The structural matrices can either be displayed with a mod.showX() method or used in further calculations as a numeric array.
